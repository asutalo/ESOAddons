## Title: Achievement Bar
## Author: HeruRaRa
## Description: Displays a progress bar at the bottom of the screen for a tracked achievement. Once an achievement is complete, the addon will either show a bar for the follow-up achievement or disappear if there aren't any.
## APIVersion: 101047
## Version: 1.0
## SavedVariables: AchievementBar_Data
